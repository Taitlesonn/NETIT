Adding a new printer

One amazing feature of Fedora and Ubuntu is their automatic detection of 
printers connected via USB and parallel port cables. If you have one of these 
printers, you most likely don’t need to add it to the system. It’s automatically 
listed under the Local Printers listing. You can just move on to the next sec-
tion to configure it.

If you’re not fortunate enough to have a USB-connected printer, you have to 
add your printer manually. Here are the steps for doing that:

 

1.  Click the New Printer button on the toolbar.

 

  The New Printer Wizard appears, as shown in Figure 18-9.
