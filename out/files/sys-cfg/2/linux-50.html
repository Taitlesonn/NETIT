Gather information by reading the screen when the computer starts. 

If your system doesn’t contain any operating systems and you don’t 
have any of your system’s documentation, you can resort to reading the 
screen as your computer starts. On some systems, the video informa-
tion is displayed from the BIOS as the computer boots. You may have to 
reboot several times to read the information if it goes by too fast. Also, 
some systems display the PCI components and their settings as the 
system is starting up. You may have to reboot several times to gather all 
the information.
