Part III: Getting Things Done 

Google Applications have a unique feature in that they all run on Google serv-
ers on the Internet. You must have an Internet connection to use the Google 
Applications.

Documents that you create in the Google Applications servers are stored in 
your own private area on the Google network. You can choose to keep them 
private or to share them — either with the whole world or with just a few of 
your closest friends!

 

To use the Google Applications, you need to create a free account on Google 
at 

google.com

. You can either sign up for a free Google Mail account that’ll 

work for all the applications, or you can sign up for individual Google applica-
tions using your own existing e-mail address.
