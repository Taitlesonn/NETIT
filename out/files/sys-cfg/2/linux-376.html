Understanding What Virtual Servers Are

In this fast-paced world of changing technology, nothing is taken for granted. 
In the old days, if you needed to run ten separate servers on your network, 
you had to go out and purchase ten separate systems, and then place them in 
a huge data center where they took up lots of space.
