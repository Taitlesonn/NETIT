Both Terminal and Konsole have a few additional features that can come in 
handy, such as cutting and pasting text and changing the color of the text 
and the background. You can even go old-school and have green text on top 
of a black background — just like the computer terminals you see in old ’70s 
movies.

 

If you use your shell prompt quite a bit like we do, it’s handy to use what’s laid 
out in Chapters 4 and 5 about adding the terminal window to your icons along 
the desktop’s menu panel.
