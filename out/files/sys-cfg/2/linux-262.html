What Sound? I Don’t Hear a Thing!

These days, most Linux distributions detect sound cards automatically 
during the installation process — and they do a great job of it. However, if 
your sound card failed the test — or you try to play sound using one of the 
software options in this chapter and it doesn’t work — use this section to 
diagnose and fix your problem.
