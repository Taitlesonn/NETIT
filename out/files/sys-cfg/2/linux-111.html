Part I: Getting Your Feet Wet 

That’s quite a collection of widgets available to play with! You can experi-
ment with widgets by adding them to your panel or desktop, and then easily 
remove them if you don’t find them useful.

To add the widget, just select the one you want, and then click the Add 
Widget button. Most widgets provide a simple control menu to modify the 
layout and behavior of the widget by either right-clicking the widget in the 
panel or hovering your mouse pointer over the widget on the desktop. From 
this menu you can move the widget, remove the widget, or (for some wid-
gets) access a Properties dialog box to configure the widget.

Getting more widgets

Besides all of the standard widgets, you can download even more. Click the 
Install New Widgets button in the Add Widgets dialog box to find these two 
options:

 ✓ 

Download New Plasma Widgets:

 Browse the online repositories for new 

widget packages.

 ✓ 

Install Widget from Local File:

 Browse to a location on your worksta-

tion to install a new widget package from a file already on your 
workstation.
