Burning Data CDs and DVDs

A CD and DVD burner is a great way to make data backups (especially if the 
disc is rewritable), save and share your digital photos, put together multime-
dia mementos or scrapbooks, and more. Many tools are available that allow 
you to burn CDs under Linux; here’s a quick look at a few of them.

The K3b package

By far the most popular CD and DVD burner used in the KDE environment 
is K3b. Unfortunately, it’s not installed by default in Fedora. If you don’t 
have it installed yet, see Chapter 16 for how to do so — the specific pack-
ages you want are 

k3b

k3b-extras
