If all you want to do is create an audio CD out of music or audio files you 
downloaded, do the following:

 

1.  Click the New Audio CD Project option in the main K3b window.

 

  The Audio CD Current Projects dialog box opens (Figure 13-13).

 

2.  In the top section, click the folder button and navigate to the files you 

want to burn onto the CD.
