Fonts

The Fonts tab allows you to customize which fonts Ubuntu uses for various 
functions within the desktop. As expected, Ubuntu provides lots of different 
fonts to choose from. Another nice feature is that you can customize how the 
fonts are rendered on your screen.

Clicking the Details button at the bottom of the Fonts tab page opens the 
Font Rendering Details dialog box, shown in Figure 4-8.

Here you can really get down to the basics of rendering fonts on your screen.
