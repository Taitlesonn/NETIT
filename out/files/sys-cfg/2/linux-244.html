Fine Art with OpenOffice.Org Draw

Whether you’re an aspiring graphic artist or just need a tool that lets you 
generate simple graphics for use on their own, in a presentation, or elsewhere, 
OpenOffice.org Draw provides a host of drawing functions. If nothing else, it’s 
a whole lot of fun to play with! Not everything in life has to be practical.
