Using Gadgets

Having Gadgets at your fingertips isn’t any fun unless you know how to use 
them. This section walks through how to use the Google Gadgets installed 
by default in the gOS desktop, how to customize them for your own environ-
ment, and how to add new Gadgets easily. These same instructions also work 
if you’ve installed Google Gadgets in another Linux distribution, although the 
default Gadgets may not be the same.
