Double-Checking Hardware 

Compatibility

If you’re installing Linux on hardware you already own, just give it a try and 
then see what does and doesn’t work. Parts of this section can help you out; 
they address fixing general hardware problems. Other chapters address 
more specific hardware issues such as sound cards (Chapter 13), wireless 
cards (Chapter 9), and so on. If you run into trouble, start in the specific sec-
tions dedicated to particular tasks, and then come here for more general help 
if you still haven’t solved the problem.
