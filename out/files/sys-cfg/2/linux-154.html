Getting Information You 

Need from Your ISP

Most reputable ISPs provide you with a customer information sheet after you 
sign up for their services. This sheet should include the following information 
(at minimum):

 ✓ 

Local telephone dial-in numbers for modem users

 ✓ 

User login name

 ✓ 

User login password

 ✓ 

E-mail address
