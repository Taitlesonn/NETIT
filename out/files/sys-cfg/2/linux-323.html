Finding More Software

What if you can’t find what you’re looking for through the official (and not so 
official) sources discussed in the previous section? Those aren’t your only 
options. Although we can’t anticipate every situation you might find yourself 
in, we can at least give you some tips on how to find extra software — and 
how to install much of it.

Here are the general steps for finding new software:

 

1.  Check the Fedora FAQ (

www.fedorafaq.org

), the Fedora News (

www.

fedoranews.org

), the Fedora Forum (

www.fedoraforum.org

), and 

other online sites that people might recommend.
