Adding an applet to the panel

The Add to Panel dialog box (see Figure 4-3) is accessible from the Panel 
menu (see the previous section). This dialog box contains a list of 

applets

 â€” 

small, specialized programs you can use to add particular functionality to a 
panel. To add one of these applets to your panel, open the dialog box, select 
the applet you want to use, and then click Add. The applet now appears on 
your panel. If you right-click the applet and choose Move, you can then slide 
the applet along your panel until you have it where you want it, and then 
click to release it.

You may have noticed that the first applet item is Custom Application 
Launcher. Using this feature, you can create a panel icon that launches your 
own program or script.

Configuring an applet

After you have an applet placed and running, you may be able to play with 
configuration options. Some of these options enable you to change what 
information is displayed. Others have a variety of look-and-feel settings.
