Tip #3: “I Told the Installer to Test My 

Graphics, and They Failed”

The installer may have guessed wrong about what hardware you have. 
Double-check the settings as best you can. If they look right, try choosing a 
lower resolution for the time being, and test again; if that fails, try a lower 
number of colors and test again. You can then try setting things back the way 
you want them after the machine is fully installed and updated, when (hope-
fully) it will have a fix available for whatever the problem might be.
