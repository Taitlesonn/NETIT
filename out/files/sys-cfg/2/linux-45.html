Determining how much space is enough for your Windows setup — you 
don’t want to shrink it so much that you run out of space in Windows — also 
involves taking a look at how you use your machine. Again, if you download 
and save a lot of multimedia stuff, it’s wise to keep enough room around to 
accommodate your downloading habit.

If you find that your Windows drives are all too full and you have no room to 
spare (or you download and save too many audio, video, or graphics files), 
it may be time to save some of your files to CDs or DVDs so you can delete 
them from your hard drive. The other option is to add a second drive to the 
computer — if you’re not comfortable doing this yourself, many stores will 
install a drive for you for a fee.
